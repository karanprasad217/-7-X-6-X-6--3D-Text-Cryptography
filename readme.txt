3D-Playfair cipher is a multiple letter encryption cipher. Digraphs (combination of 2 characters) of the plaintext are treated as a single
unit and converted into corresponding ciphertext digraphs and vice-versa. This 3D-Playfair cipher supports all ASCII Characters including
26 English alphabets {A-Z}, 10 digits {0-9} and all special characters including ♪, ♫, ☼, ►, ☼, ↓, →, ∟, $, §, ▬, ↨, ¶, ▲, ▼, !, #, |, ~
, Ç, ü, é, â, å, ç, è, ï, Ä etc.  3D because, project uses 7 X 6 X 6 matrix structure to build ciphertext. The time complexity and
performance of our implemented cipher is far better than any other proposed/implemented Playfair Cipher. This project can be accessed over
